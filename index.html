Historically Funny.

Hysterically Inaccurate.

Textbooks are so boring, but history is full of crazy characters! We've taken it upon ourselves to rewrite a high school US History textbook. Every show starts with the audience selecting a page from an existing textbook, which determines the chapter of the book we will rewrite that night. The rest of the show is dedicated to making up funnier versions of history live on stage! From time to time, we will ask you to help us rewrite history. Historical accuracy not guaranteed.
