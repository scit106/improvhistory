Historically Funny.

Hysterically Inaccurate.

An Improvised People's History is a fully improvised show which invites the audience to collaborate on a new chapter in a US history textbook. Famous (and not-so-famous) figures are brought back to life in front of your eyes. It all begins when the audience picks a page from a real US history textbook, then hilarity ensues as the improvisers rewrite that chapter in history with their own (mis)interpretations. Most performances are at ImprovBoston in Cambridge, MA, but the cast has been known to hitch up the wagons and explore new lands! Be sure to get in touch if you would like to know where our manifest destiny takes us next.
